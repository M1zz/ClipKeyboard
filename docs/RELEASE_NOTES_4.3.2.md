- 콤보는 첫 번째 값을 미리 보여줍니다, 콤보 메모의 힌트와 미리보기에 개수만이 아니라 첫 단계 값이 함께 표시되어 어떤 콤보인지 바로 알아볼 수 있습니다
- 힌트가 부담스러우면 끌 수 있습니다, 설정 → 메모 표시의 "메모 내용 힌트" 토글 하나로 앱 카드와 키보드 모두 한꺼번에 켜고 끕니다 — 보안 메모의 내용은 어디에서도 표시되지 않아요
- 검색 키보드가 제때 내려갑니다, 메인 화면 검색 중 다른 곳을 탭해도 키보드가 안 내려가던 문제를 고쳤습니다

English

- Memo cards now gently whisper their contents, once a card has been on screen for about two seconds, a single line of its content forms below the title as if a blur is lifting, lingers briefly, then scatters away — it repeats from time to time while the app is open, and every card breathes at a slightly different rhythm so the screen never blinks all at once
- On the keyboard, names briefly become contents, after a memo key has been visible for about two seconds, its name slowly melts into the content and back — so even on the compact keyboard you know what's inside before you tap
- Choose the exact line to show, when creating or editing a memo, write a "content hint" and it appears instead of the automatic summary — a "Show with the name on keyboard" toggle lets you decide per memo whether the keyboard shows it too
- Combos preview their first value, combo hints and previews now lead with the first step's value, not just a count, so you can tell combos apart at a glance
- Turn hints off if they're not your thing, one "Memo Content Hints" toggle in Settings → Memo Display controls both the app cards and the keyboard — and secure memo contents are never shown anywhere
- The search keyboard dismisses properly again, fixed an issue where tapping elsewhere while searching on the main screen wouldn't dismiss the keyboard

App Store Summary (English)

• Memo content hints — after a card sits on screen for ~2 seconds, a line of content gently forms below the title and scatters away, repeating occasionally with each card on its own rhythm
• On the keyboard, names slowly swap to contents and back, so you know what's inside before tapping
• Write your own "content hint" per memo — shown instead of the auto summary, with a per-memo keyboard toggle
• Combos lead with their first value; secure memo contents are never shown anywhere
• One settings toggle controls hints across both the app and the keyboard
• Fixed the main-screen search keyboard not dismissing when tapping elsewhere
